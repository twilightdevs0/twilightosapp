Privacy Policy
Effective date: June 13, 2026
Introduction
Twilight OS is a reflective journaling, awareness, action, and continuity-of-thought app built around Applied Lucidity.
This Privacy Policy explains what information Twilight OS collects, how it is used, how it may be shared, and what choices users have. Twilight OS is designed to be privacy-conscious and local-first where possible.
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact:
twilight.devs.0@gmail.com
Information We Collect
Twilight OS may collect or store the following types of information, depending on which features you use.
Reflection and Journal Content
The app may store reflections, prompt responses, generated Twilight insights, Dayside actions, returning inquiries, themes, reports, and related app activity.
Current reflection data is intended to be stored locally on the user's device unless the user chooses to export or share it.
Account Information
If account features are enabled and you choose to create or use an account, Twilight OS may collect information needed to maintain that account, such as:
Full name or preferred name
Email address
Account status
Subscription or entitlement status
Support-related communications
This information is used for account maintenance, authentication, support, subscription access, and service administration.
Subscription and Purchase Information
If premium features or subscriptions are enabled, subscription status may be processed through Google Play or another authorized billing provider. Twilight OS does not receive full payment card details from Google Play.
Support and Error Information
If you contact support or choose to share diagnostic information, Twilight OS may receive information you provide, such as your email address, support message, app version, local error details, or relevant exported logs.
Local error logging is optional. Logs are not uploaded automatically.
AI-Related Information
Twilight OS may later offer optional AI-assisted features. If those features are enabled, reflection content or structured prompt context may be sent to an AI provider only after user-facing disclosure and consent.
Current local preview tools may show what a future AI request would look like without sending it.
How We Use Information
We use information to:
Provide reflection, journaling, action, report, and continuity features
Maintain user accounts, if account features are enabled
Manage subscriptions or premium access, if enabled
Provide support when requested
Improve reliability and troubleshoot user-reported issues
Preserve app safety, privacy, and user control
Enable optional AI-assisted features only when disclosed and accepted
Data Sharing
Twilight OS does not sell personal data.
We may share or process data only in limited situations:
With service providers needed to operate account, subscription, support, hosting, or optional AI features
With Google Play or authorized billing services for subscription and purchase handling
When the user chooses to export, save, or share their own data
When required by law, valid legal process, security, fraud prevention, or protection of rights
Any third-party service used for future account, subscription, support, analytics, or AI functionality should be disclosed before use.
Data Storage and Security
Twilight OS is designed to keep reflection data local where possible. Data stored on the device may be protected by the device's operating system, lock screen, storage protections, and user-controlled backups.
For account, subscription, support, or future cloud-connected features, reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards will be used to protect personal information. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure.
Data Retention
Local reflection data remains on the device until the user deletes it, clears app data, uninstalls the app, or uses app features that remove it.
Account-related information may be retained while the account is active and for a reasonable period afterward as needed for support, legal, security, fraud prevention, billing, or recordkeeping purposes.
Support communications and diagnostic information may be retained as needed to respond to the request and improve reliability.
Data Deletion
Users may delete local reflections and app data through available app features or device controls.
If account features are enabled, users may request account deletion by contacting:
twilight.devs.0@gmail.com
When an account deletion request is processed, associated account data will be deleted unless retention is required for legal, security, fraud prevention, billing, dispute resolution, or other legitimate purposes.
Children
Twilight OS is not intended for children under 13. The app is intended for a general audience interested in reflection, journaling, and personal organization.
Health, Medical, and Diagnostic Boundaries
Twilight OS is not a medical, mental health, crisis, diagnostic, or emergency service. The app may support reflection and self-observation, but it does not replace qualified professional advice, diagnosis, treatment, or emergency support.
If you are in immediate danger or experiencing a crisis, contact local emergency services or a qualified crisis support resource.
International Users
Privacy and data protection rights may vary depending on where you live. Users may contact Twilight OS at the email above with privacy questions, access requests, deletion requests, or other privacy-related inquiries.
Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated as Twilight OS adds account, subscription, support, AI, cloud, or other connected features. Updated versions will be made available through the app and/or the public privacy policy URL.
