Twilight OS helps you observe what is present, reflect with clarity, choose one grounded action, and return later to see how your thinking has evolved.
Built around the philosophy of Applied Lucidity, the app separates reflection from action:
Nightside helps you explore prompts, patterns, and questions.
Twilight turns reflection into insight, one practical Dayside action, and one returning inquiry.
Dayside helps you track actions in the real world.
Archive and Journey help you revisit sessions, threads, themes, and reports.
Twilight OS is designed to reveal, not direct. It does not replace professional advice, diagnosis, or qualified support. It helps you keep your own thought process visible, organized, and actionable.
Current features include guided reflections, reflection history, continuing threads, Journey patterns, practice suggestions, exportable reports, appearance settings, and local privacy-focused controls.
